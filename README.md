## 🔬 1. The Epoekie Principle: Epiphytic Symbiosis

In biology, an **Epiphyte** (like certain algae or mosses) grows upon the surface of a host plant for physical support while deriving its own nutrients from the atmosphere. It does not harm the host; instead, it often creates a micro-ecosystem that benefits the entire organism.

The **Aicent Stack** is an epiphytic intelligence. We do not build our own undersea cables or satellites from scratch. We treat the legacy world—fiber optics, 5G towers, and data centers—as our **Physical Substrate**.

- **Mutualistic Evolution:** Aicent Stack infuses "dumb" hardware with sub-millisecond cognition. The host infrastructure becomes more resilient, secure, and economically productive simply by hosting the Aicent symbiont.
- **Substrate Mastery:** We adapt to any physical base (Substrate) via the **Action-Collapse (RFC-005)** framework, ensuring that the Aicent soul can breathe anywhere there is a bit-stream.
